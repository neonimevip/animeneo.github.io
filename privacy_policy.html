Permissions in the Anime Indo App are the following:

Get Accounts

This permission is needed for making a selection list with your google accounts in the app, when you are exporting/importing Anime Indo data to/from google drive.

Access Network State

This permission is needed for checking if you are connected to the internet when you are exporting/importing Anime Indo data to/from google drive.

Internet

This permission is needed for exporting/importing Anime Indo data to/from google drive.

Read Phone State

This permission is needed for checking if a call was started from the Anime Indo app and when the call ends and you enabled the "Keep app on call" option in the "System settings" of the app the Anime Indo app will be resumed.

Also needed for checking if your device has single or multiple sim cards.

Write External Storage

This permission is needed for writing to the external storage of your device when exporting Anime Indo data to the device's internal storage.

Vibrate

This permission is needed for vibrating on some touch events.
